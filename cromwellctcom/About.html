About Us

Welcome to Cromwell, known as the "Crossroads of Connecticut." Nestled near the heart of the state, Cromwell spans 13.5 square miles and is conveniently situated just 14 miles south of Hartford and 28 miles north of New Haven. The town's eastern boundary is defined by the scenic Connecticut River, offering stunning views, vibrant wildlife, and abundant outdoor recreational activities such as boating, fishing, and hiking.

At the southern tip of Cromwell, you'll find the historic downtown Riverport district, home to charming retail shops, inviting restaurants, and picturesque riverfront parks that capture the beauty and heritage of our community. The district’s well-preserved architecture and peaceful walking trails provide a glimpse into Cromwell’s rich history and serve as a gathering spot for both residents and visitors.

Cromwell is also known for its commitment to preserving green spaces, with numerous parks and open areas providing a serene environment for relaxation, sports, and family activities. Pierson Park, one of the town's most beloved public spaces, regularly hosts community events, athletic games, and social gatherings, making it a central hub of activity. The TPC River Highlands golf course, located just minutes from downtown, hosts the annual Travelers Championship, attracting professional golfers and sports enthusiasts from around the world.

In addition to its natural beauty, Cromwell boasts a strong sense of community, with excellent schools, family-oriented neighborhoods, and a range of cultural and recreational opportunities. Whether you're exploring the vibrant local business scene or attending one of the town’s seasonal festivals, Cromwell offers a welcoming atmosphere that reflects the warmth and spirit of its residents.

With its perfect blend of history, natural beauty, and modern amenities, Cromwell is a destination that offers something for everyone. We invite you to experience all that our town has to offer.
